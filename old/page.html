How to use Breeze

Shortcuts A shortcut redirects you directly to the associated link. e.g., gmail redirects you to https://gmail.com
Tags A tag takes you to the associated link but replaces {{x}} with the rest of the input. e.g., reddit programming takes you to https://www.reddit.com/r/programming (https://www.reddit.com/r/{{x}} )
URLs Writing a valid URL redirects you to that URL. Every URL is saved and shows up as a suggestion the next time you write something similar.
You can press TAB to use auto-complete at any time. You can choose the target using:
    Return: open in this tab
    Ctrl/Cmd + Return: open in new tab
    Shift + Return: open and jump to new tab
    Alt + Return: open in incognito mode
Every Shortcut, Tag and URL can be deleted and new ones can be added in the settings at any time. You can access the settings page by clicking in the Breeze logo located in the extensions area (top right of the browser window). Don't forget to refresh the page after you make any changes.
